# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity. The full text of the
Contributor Covenant v2.1 applies.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to **conduct@zeam.app**. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

See <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/> for
the full text.
